Our Story

Burger House was founded in 1999 by two passionate chefs, John and Jane, who believed in creating something special for their community. They knew that every burger should taste like home, and they wanted to make that happen. With a commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction, Burger House has become a beloved local favorite.
